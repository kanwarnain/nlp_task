I forgot to bring my mobile phone, or Shell Go+ card/key fob. Is there another way to have my reward redeemed?

  • 14 September 2021 19:51
  • Updated

A reward cannot be redeemed without presenting the Shell Go+ App from your mobile phone. Or if you activate the reward on the website and then scan your Keyfob or Shell Go+ card.
